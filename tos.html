1. What VeilPay is

VeilPay is a non-custodial invoicing and payment interface built on Solana. It lets freelancers generate invoices and receive real USDC payments on Solana mainnet, submitted via VeilPay's Smart TX Stack (dynamic Jito tips and AI-assisted retries) so payments land reliably even under network congestion. VeilPay does not hold, custody, or have access to your funds at any point — all transfers happen wallet-to-wallet, approved directly by you.
