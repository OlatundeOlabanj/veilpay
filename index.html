Built on real infrastructure, not marketing copy — dynamic Jito tips and an AI retry agent replace the guesswork of a plain wallet transfer.

Built to Land

Every payment is submitted as a Jito bundle with a live-market tip. Under congestion, that's the difference between a payment that lands and one that stalls.

AI-Assisted Retries

If a transaction stalls, a Groq AI agent — not a hardcoded if/else — decides whether to retry and at what tip, based on live network conditions.

Shareable Invoice Links

Generate a payment link for any client. They connect, pay, and USDC lands directly in your wallet — no separate claim step.

How It Works

Three steps to a landed payment.

01

Client Pays

Your client approves one signature in their wallet — that's the whole payment flow on their end.

02

Smart Submission

VeilPay's Smart TX Stack submits it as a Jito bundle with a live-market tip, with a Groq AI agent deciding on retries if the network's congested.

03

Landed On-Chain

USDC settles on Solana mainnet directly into your wallet — 98% to you, 2% platform fee. No separate claim step.
